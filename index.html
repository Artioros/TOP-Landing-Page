Let's talk about elephants

Did you know that elephants can't jump? Elephants are the only mammal that can't jump. This is due to their size, weight, and bone structure.

How much do you know about animals?

The hyena's characteristic "laugh" is a vocalization used for communication.

Some penguins hold their breath for over 20 minutes while diving deep for food.

Gorillas are incredibly strong, a silverback can lift up to 10 times their own body weight.

Red panda cubs are born in tree hollows and stay with their mother for about a year.
